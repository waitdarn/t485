Website Introduction

Hello, .

Welcome to Troop 485! This page will get you started using the online rescources for T485. The main online rescources for T485 are our Google Groups page/email, this website, and the T485 Slack.

The T485 Google Group/Email

Google groups is basically a mailing list for all of the members of a Google Group. Whenever anyone emails something to t485[a T]googlegroups.com, the email is forwarded to all members of that Google Group. In gmail, Google Group emails are sorted in the Forums Tab(if you use tabs in gmail to sort) There is also an online service at groups.google.com in which you can view and search all the past emails sent to the T485 Google Group.

To get started, just click here, and request to join the T485 google group, then send me, Jeffrey Meng( jeffkmeng[a T]gmail.com) an email so he can allow your request to be accepted, and also so he can authorize your email for the T485 website.

The T485 website

You are currently viewing the Troop 485 website, www.t485.org. You can get to this website by going to https://t485.org, which will redirect you to https://www.t485.org
To use most of the features described in the below section, you will need to be a part of the T485 Google Group, and wait for your email to be authorized to view parts of the website.

The directory, avaliable in the Navbar(Navagation Bar), contains contact information for every scout in our troop. To get to the directory, click the link in the Navbar at the top of every page, and login. You will be automatically redirected to the login page if you are not already logged in. Click on the Login with Google button and log in with the Google account you recieve your Google Group emails in. You will then be able to search the directoy to get contact information for any scout in T485.

You will also need to add your own contact information. At the bottom of the directory page there is a button called Show spreadsheet. Choose the tab for your patrol, and click the link that says edit spreadsheet and add your contact information in a new line.

The meeting minutes page, avaliable in the navbar under resources, which requires login, contains links to all the meeting minutes for each troop meeting. If you miss a troop meeting, you may want to check the meeting minutes to see if there is any important information from that meeting that you missed.

The calendar page, which does not require login, contains a Google Calendar for most of the events in Troop 485.


The T485 Slack

Slack is a instant-messenger style communication tool. You can get there at t485.slack.com, however, you will need to ask your patrol leader to send you an invitation first.

Thanks for taking the time to read this page fully. If you have any questions, or wish to offer feedback on this page or the website in general, please email it to Jeffrey Meng( jeffkmeng[a T]gmail.com).
